Nail Services

Nail Enhancements

We offer two types of nail enhancements: Artificial Nails and Dipping Powder.

Artificial Nails

A classic system which involves applying a mixture of liquid and powder acrylic to the nails. Once cured, the nails are filed to the desired shape and thickness. After which, the nails will be buffed smooth and polish can be applied.

A nail enhancement system which first begins with an application of resin "glue" to the nail. The finger is then dipped into colored acrylic powder and the process is repeated until the desired thickness is achieved.
